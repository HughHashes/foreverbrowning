Welcome, family and friends!

March 17, 2018 -- Powhatan, VA



Adam Browning

I remember the exact moment I knew I was in love with Lindsay. It was Memorial Day 2015 and we were walking around the Congressional Cemetery looking for a path that she had restored on an outing while with Deloitte. There was a moment where she was walking, looking at the tombstones, with the wind blowing her dress and her hair perfectly, and that was it. It only took me 8 more months to tell her...

Lindsay Brannan

I had loved Adam for a while by late 2015, but I had been too chicken to say anything. One day, while driving to work I noticed a weird noise in the car. When I got to work, I called Adam to see if he thought it was a big deal (he did not think it was). We were hanging up, and just like I had said it every day of my life, I said “Bye, have a good day. I love you!” I immediately hung up the phone on him and later that night we finally said it to each other. Now I do say it everyday, and it is just as natural. That noise in the car never came back though…





Our Story

The last three years have had countless adventures and memories, but here are some of the highlights:

  • First We Meet

    June 11, 2014

    When Adam and Lindsay first met in preservice training for DC Teaching Fellows, Lindsay definitely thought Adam was a huge tool. It wasn’t until they started teaching at the same school and Adam shared his watermelon that she came around. (With a little convincing from Taylor!!)

  • First Daste

    July 18, 2014

    A group of teachers went out to the Nationals game after school on Friday, and Adam did everything in his power (including calling in recruiters, thanks Jen!) to get Lindsay to join them. Finally she decided to go and they went off on their own to find hot dogs and Dip n’ Dots! In the end, they knew they needed to keep it professional. Spoiler alert: They kissed!

  • In A Relationship

    February 18, 2015

    They were planning their first trip together - Spring Break in Nashville! Adam reached out to their AirBnB host, Jim, and started the paragraph, “My girlfriend and I…” With Lindsay right beside him, her response was, “so… I’m your girlfriend?” From that point until Spring Break 2017, she was!

  • Engaged

    April 17, 2017

    Adam and Lindsay were three days into their Ireland trip when they went to the Cliffs of Moher. They hiked along the cliffs until they got to the perfect spot for Adam to scare the heck out of Lindsay by peering over the edge. However, when he turned around, he was on one knee with the ring box in his hand. (See above for her reaction…)
